🛠️✨ **Build submission updates!** We squashed a bug that was blocking members from submitting builds ([#42](https://github.com/cyberdeck-club/cyberdeck.club/issues/42), [#43](https://github.com/cyberdeck-club/cyberdeck.club/issues/43)) — huge thanks to **Pineheap** and **Goosie** for catching that one! Members can now submit builds as intended (that's how you become a Maker, after all 💖).

We also added a **confirmation page** after submission so you know your build made it through, and reviewers can now view the **full build detail page** before approving — photos, BOM, wiring diagrams, everything ([#40](https://github.com/cyberdeck-club/cyberdeck.club/issues/40)). Thank you **therebelrobot** for pushing for that! 💜

🆕 **"My Builds" page** — Find all your builds (published, pending review, needing revision) in one place at `/builds/mine`. There's a link in your user menu!

🔄 **Build feedback loop** — If a moderator requests revisions, you'll get an email with their constructive feedback and a link to edit and resubmit. When your build is approved and goes live, you'll get a celebratory email too! 🎉

📬 **Announcement emails** — Published announcements now get emailed to all community members so nobody misses important updates.

These improvements all came from YOUR feedback — keep it coming!
